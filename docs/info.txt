## SIZE GUIDE

### Bra Sizing Guide

**How to Measure:**
1. **Band Size:** Measure snugly around your ribcage, directly under your bust
2. **Bust Size:** Measure around the fullest part of your bust
3. **Calculate:** Subtract band measurement from bust measurement

**Size Chart:**

| Band Size (inches) | Cup Difference | Cup Size |
|-------------------|----------------|----------|
| 28-29 | 0-1" | AA/A |
| 30-31 | 1-2" | A/B |
| 32-33 | 2-3" | B/C |
| 34-35 | 3-4" | C/D |
| 36-37 | 4-5" | D/DD |

## FABRICS & MATERIALS

### Our Fabric Philosophy
At Arroca, we believe exceptional comfort begins with exceptional materials. Every fabric in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, performance, and luxurious feel.

### Premium Materials We Use

**Italian Microfiber**
Silky-smooth, moisture-wicking, and incredibly durable. Our Italian microfiber feels weightless against skin while providing excellent shape retention.

**French Lace**
Delicate yet resilient, our French Leaver's lace adds romantic detail without sacrificing comfort. Each pattern is designed to lay flat and move with your body.

**Modal & Bamboo Blends**
Sustainably sourced, breathable, and softer than cotton. Perfect for everyday wear and sensitive skin.

**Technical Mesh**
Engineered for our Active Collection, this breathable fabric offers compression and support while keeping you cool.

**Silk Blends**
Temperature-regulating and hypoallergenic, our silk blends bring timeless luxury to loungewear and sleepwear.

**Sustainable Practices**
We prioritize OEKO-TEX certified fabrics, water-conscious dyeing processes, and partnerships with mills committed to environmental responsibility.
